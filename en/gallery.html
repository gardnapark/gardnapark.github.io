Gardna Park

Gardna Park is an area on Lake Gardno, near the Baltic Sea with wide sandy beaches and sea climate

The main attraction is the proximity of the Baltic Sea. But there are also other possibilities for recreation: a paradise for anglers, for cyclists there are marked out "Routes of rolled tracks" on the former railway embankments, and for those who love history, the oldest water power trail in Europe from the beginning of the 20th century, and many other attractions.
